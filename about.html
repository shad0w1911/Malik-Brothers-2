ABOUT US

Who We Are

MALIK BROTHERS was founded in 1980 by Mr. Malik Zafar Mahmood with his brothers ( Malik Hamid Mahmood & Malik Shafqat Mahmood to cater the requirements of the local market of Karachi Pakistan.

In the year 2005, Malik Brothers expanded their market and open new retail points in Malir Cantt. and Super highway, Karachi Pakistan In 2011, Malik Brothers is opening new branch in Defence area (Bukhari Commercial) DHA phase 6 Karachi Pakistan.

Beyond above, Malik Brothers has planned to expand their wings in the field of Real Estate, Transport, Civil services, Interior designing and Engineering area.
